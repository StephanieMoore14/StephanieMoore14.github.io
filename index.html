About Me

As a Product Manager specializing in health technology, I'm passionate about creating digital health solutions that make a real difference in people's lives. With expertise in continuous glucose monitoring (CGM), wearable technology, and health data analytics, I work at the intersection of innovation and practical healthcare delivery.

My work focuses on understanding complex health ecosystems, conducting competitive analyses, and translating insights into actionable product strategies. I believe in data-driven decision making combined with genuine empathy for the user experience.

Mega-Meal Competitive Review

Comprehensive analysis of how leading CGM and nutrition apps handle complex meal scenarios, including overlapping meals and extended eating periods.

Key Findings:

  • CGM-based platforms utilize real-time glucose metrics (AUC, peaks, slopes)
  • Meal timing and sequencing significantly impact algorithmic approaches
  • User-facing messaging varies widely across platforms
